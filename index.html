About us

We are a team of passionate agriculturist and Fruits and vegetables suppliers, we have been supplying Fresh vegetables and fruits from India. We have expanded our operation into frozen fruits and vegetables to cater to a larger customer base of the Retail and HORECA segment.

We are a highly professional and experienced team of Agronomist, Food Technologist, quality assurance which gives us an edge to cater to highly quality-conscious markets.

We procure the IQF Frozen Fruits and vegetables from BRC/HACCP/HALAL Certified units .Our team of quality assurance do the internal inspection of the plants to verify the hygiene and quality of the product to ensure supply of best quality of frozen vegetables and fruits.

Our Fresh and IQF Frozen Fruits and Vegetables are available in customized packing and at highly competitive rates.
